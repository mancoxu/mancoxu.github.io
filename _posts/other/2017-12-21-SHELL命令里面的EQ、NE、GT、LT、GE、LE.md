`EQ(eq)` 就是 EQUAL **等于**

`NQ(nq)` 就是 NOT EQUAL **不等于**

`GT(gt)` 就是 GREATER THAN **大于**

`LT(lt)` 就是 LESS THAN **小于**

`GE(ge)` 就是 GREATER THAN OR EQUAL **大于等于** 

`LE(le)` 就是 LESS THAN OR EQUAL **小于等于**
